# Quest System Documentation

## Overview

The Quest System provides structured challenges that guide users through learning objectives while rewarding them with XP, coins, and exclusive items.

## Quest Types

### 1. Daily Quests

**Characteristics:**
- Reset every 24 hours at midnight
- 3 active quests per day
- Simple, achievable goals
- Encourage daily engagement

**Examples:**
- Daily Login (25 XP, 10 coins)
- Complete a Lesson (100 XP, 20 coins)
- Review 20 Flashcards (75 XP, 15 coins)
- Take a Quiz (150 XP, 30 coins)
- Help the Community (100 XP, 25 coins)
- Practice Coding (200 XP, 40 coins)

**Benefits:**
- Quick XP gains
- Establishes daily habits
- Low time commitment
- Instant gratification

### 2. Weekly Quests

**Characteristics:**
- Reset every Monday at midnight
- 5 active quests per week
- Moderate difficulty
- Require sustained effort

**Examples:**

#### Weekly Consistency
- **Objective**: Complete at least one lesson every day this week
- **Requirement**: 7-day lesson streak
- **Rewards**: 500 XP, 100 coins

#### Quiz Champion
- **Objective**: Complete 10 quizzes with 80%+ score this week
- **Requirement**: 10 high-score quizzes
- **Rewards**: 1,000 XP, 200 coins

#### Review Champion
- **Objective**: Review 200 flashcards this week
- **Requirement**: 200 flashcard reviews
- **Rewards**: 750 XP, 150 coins

#### Coding Champion
- **Objective**: Complete 15 coding exercises this week
- **Requirement**: 15 exercises
- **Rewards**: 1,500 XP, 300 coins, Small XP Booster

#### Community Contributor
- **Objective**: Help 5 other users this week
- **Requirement**: 5 helpful answers
- **Rewards**: 500 XP, 100 coins

**Benefits:**
- Higher rewards than daily quests
- Promotes weekly planning
- Includes bonus items
- Builds momentum

### 3. Tutorial Quests

**Characteristics:**
- One-time completion
- Guides new users
- Sequential unlocking
- Forms quest chain

**Quest Chain:**

#### 1. Getting Started
- **Objective**: Complete your first lesson
- **Requirement**: 1 lesson
- **Rewards**: 200 XP, 50 coins

#### 2. Test Your Knowledge
- **Objective**: Take your first quiz
- **Requirement**: 1 quiz
- **Rewards**: 200 XP, 50 coins
- **Prerequisite**: Getting Started

#### 3. Memory Training
- **Objective**: Review your first 10 flashcards
- **Requirement**: 10 flashcard reviews
- **Rewards**: 150 XP, 40 coins
- **Prerequisite**: Test Your Knowledge

#### 4. Hands-On Practice
- **Objective**: Complete your first coding exercise
- **Requirement**: 1 exercise
- **Rewards**: 300 XP, 75 coins, Beginner Badge
- **Prerequisite**: Memory Training

#### 5. Join the Community
- **Objective**: Make your first forum post
- **Requirement**: 1 forum post
- **Rewards**: 150 XP, 50 coins
- **Prerequisite**: Hands-On Practice

**Benefits:**
- Smooth onboarding
- Feature discovery
- Early engagement
- Learning reinforcement

### 4. Story Quests

**Characteristics:**
- Narrative-driven
- Multi-chapter progression
- Technology-specific
- Long-term commitment

**Playwright Journey:**

#### Chapter 1: Beginning
- **Objective**: Begin your Playwright adventure
- **Requirements**: 5 Playwright lessons
- **Rewards**: 750 XP, 150 coins

#### Chapter 2: Mastery
- **Objective**: Master Playwright selectors
- **Requirements**:
  - 10 Playwright lessons
  - 5 Playwright exercises
- **Rewards**: 1,500 XP, 300 coins, Playwright Specialist Badge
- **Prerequisite**: Chapter 1

**Selenium Journey:**

#### Chapter 1: Beginning
- **Objective**: Start your Selenium adventure
- **Requirements**: 5 Selenium lessons
- **Rewards**: 750 XP, 150 coins

#### Chapter 2: Mastery
- **Objective**: Master Selenium WebDriver
- **Requirements**:
  - 10 Selenium lessons
  - 5 Selenium exercises
- **Rewards**: 1,500 XP, 300 coins, Selenium Expert Badge
- **Prerequisite**: Chapter 1

**Benefits:**
- Structured learning path
- Technology specialization
- Exclusive badges
- Narrative engagement

### 5. Seasonal Quests

**Characteristics:**
- Limited-time availability
- Themed around holidays/events
- Epic difficulty
- Exclusive rewards

**Example: Winter Learning Challenge**

- **Duration**: December 1 - January 31
- **Objective**: Complete the winter challenge event
- **Requirements**:
  - Complete 25 lessons
  - Complete 10 quizzes
  - Maintain 14-day streak
- **Rewards**:
  - 5,000 XP
  - 1,000 coins
  - Winter 2024 Badge (exclusive)
  - Large XP Booster

**Benefits:**
- Time-limited exclusivity
- Unique cosmetic items
- Community events
- Higher stakes engagement

## Quest Difficulties

### Easy
- **Time**: 5-15 minutes
- **XP**: 50-200
- **Coins**: 10-50
- **Examples**: Daily login, single lesson completion

### Medium
- **Time**: 15-60 minutes
- **XP**: 200-1,000
- **Coins**: 50-200
- **Examples**: Multiple quizzes, daily challenges, helping others

### Hard
- **Time**: 1-5 hours
- **XP**: 1,000-2,500
- **Coins**: 200-500
- **Examples**: Weekly challenges, multiple exercises, high scores

### Epic
- **Time**: 5+ hours or days/weeks
- **XP**: 2,500-10,000
- **Coins**: 500-2,000
- **Examples**: Seasonal events, story quest chains, major milestones

## Quest Mechanics

### Starting Quests

1. **Auto-Start**: Daily and tutorial quests start automatically
2. **Manual Start**: Story and some weekly quests require manual activation
3. **Prerequisites**: Some quests locked until prerequisites completed

### Progress Tracking

- Real-time progress updates
- Multiple requirement tracking
- Percentage completion
- Visual progress bars
- Notifications for milestones

### Completion

- Automatic detection when requirements met
- Instant reward distribution
- Completion notification
- Quest history tracking
- Statistics updates

### Quest Limits

- **Active Daily Quests**: 3 concurrent
- **Active Weekly Quests**: 5 concurrent
- **Active Story Quests**: Unlimited (as unlocked)
- **Active Tutorial Quests**: 1 at a time (sequential)

## Quest Strategies

### For Beginners
1. Complete all tutorial quests first
2. Focus on daily quests for consistency
3. Start one story quest line
4. Don't overcommit to weekly quests initially

### For Intermediate Users
1. Complete all daily quests every day
2. Target 3-4 weekly quests per week
3. Progress through story quest chains
4. Participate in seasonal events

### For Advanced Users
1. Maintain 100% daily quest completion
2. Complete all weekly quests
3. Finish all story quest lines
4. Lead in seasonal quest competitions
5. Help others complete their quests

## Quest Chains

### Definition
A series of quests where each quest unlocks the next.

### Benefits
- Guided progression
- Incremental difficulty
- Story continuity
- Cumulative rewards

### Example Chain: Full Onboarding
```
Getting Started (Tutorial 1)
  ↓
Test Your Knowledge (Tutorial 2)
  ↓
Memory Training (Tutorial 3)
  ↓
Hands-On Practice (Tutorial 4)
  ↓
Join the Community (Tutorial 5)
```

Total Rewards: 1,000 XP, 265 coins, Beginner Badge

## API Integration

### Get Daily Quests
```javascript
GET /api/gamification/quests/daily

Response: [
  {
    questId: "daily_login",
    name: "Daily Login",
    progress: [{ currentValue: 1, targetValue: 1 }],
    completed: true,
    rewards: { xp: 25, coins: 10 }
  },
  ...
]
```

### Get All Quests
```javascript
GET /api/gamification/quests?type=weekly&category=learning

Response: [
  {
    questId: "weekly_lesson_streak",
    type: "weekly",
    category: "learning",
    active: true,
    progress: [{ currentValue: 3, targetValue: 7 }],
    ...
  },
  ...
]
```

### Start Quest
```javascript
POST /api/gamification/quests/:questId/start

Response: {
  success: true,
  message: "Quest started",
  data: { activeQuests: [...] }
}
```

### Quest Progress Auto-Updates
Quest progress updates automatically when relevant activities occur:
- Lesson completion → Updates lesson-based quests
- Quiz completion → Updates quiz quests
- Flashcard review → Updates review quests
- etc.

## Best Practices

### For Platform Admins
1. **Balance Difficulty**: Ensure quests match user skill levels
2. **Reward Fairly**: XP/coins should reflect time investment
3. **Variety**: Offer diverse quest types
4. **Seasonal Content**: Regular new seasonal quests
5. **Monitor Completion**: Track completion rates, adjust difficulty
6. **Bug Testing**: Test quest triggers thoroughly

### For Users
1. **Daily Consistency**: Complete daily quests every day
2. **Plan Weekly**: Choose achievable weekly quests
3. **Don't Rush**: Focus on learning, not just completion
4. **Variety**: Try different quest types
5. **Community**: Participate in social quests
6. **Seasonal**: Don't miss time-limited quests

## Troubleshooting

### Quest Not Starting
- Check prerequisites completed
- Verify quest not already active
- Ensure cooldown period passed (for repeatable quests)

### Progress Not Updating
- Confirm activity matches quest requirement
- Check API connectivity
- Verify quest is in active list

### Quest Not Completing
- Review all requirements met
- Check for hidden conditions
- Verify backend quest logic
