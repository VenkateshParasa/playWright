# Competition System Rules and Guidelines

## Overview

Competitions provide structured challenges where users compete for prizes, rankings, and exclusive rewards. This document outlines all competition types, rules, and mechanics.

## Competition Types

### 1. Weekly Challenges
- **Duration**: Monday 00:00 to Sunday 23:59
- **Scope**: Platform-wide
- **Entry**: Free
- **Focus**: Varied activities
- **Prizes**: XP, coins, badges

### 2. Monthly Tournaments
- **Duration**: 1st to last day of month
- **Scope**: Platform-wide
- **Entry**: May require entry fee
- **Focus**: Comprehensive performance
- **Prizes**: Major XP/coins, exclusive items, titles

### 3. Head-to-Head (1v1)
- **Duration**: 1-7 days
- **Scope**: Two participants
- **Entry**: Challenge invitation
- **Focus**: Direct competition
- **Prizes**: Winner takes prize pool

### 4. Team Competitions
- **Duration**: Variable
- **Scope**: Teams of 3-10 users
- **Entry**: Team registration
- **Focus**: Collaborative scoring
- **Prizes**: Distributed to team members

### 5. Speed Challenges
- **Duration**: 1-24 hours
- **Scope**: All participants
- **Entry**: Free, open enrollment
- **Focus**: Complete X in Y time
- **Prizes**: Based on completion speed

### 6. Accuracy Challenges
- **Duration**: 1 week
- **Scope**: All participants
- **Entry**: Free
- **Focus**: High-quality performance
- **Prizes**: Based on accuracy metrics

### 7. Quiz Battles
- **Duration**: Real-time (5-15 minutes)
- **Scope**: 2-100 participants
- **Entry**: Quick match or invite
- **Focus**: Speed + accuracy
- **Prizes**: Instant rewards

## Scoring Systems

### XP-Based Scoring
- **Metric**: Total XP earned during competition
- **Activities**: All XP-earning activities count
- **Multipliers**: Boosters apply
- **Example**: User with most XP wins

### Count-Based Scoring
- **Metric**: Number of activities completed
- **Activities**: Specific to competition (lessons, quizzes, etc.)
- **Quality**: Minimum thresholds may apply
- **Example**: Complete most lessons

### Accuracy-Based Scoring
- **Metric**: Average score across activities
- **Activities**: Quizzes, exercises, reviews
- **Calculation**: (Sum of scores) / (Number of attempts)
- **Example**: Highest average quiz score

### Speed-Based Scoring
- **Metric**: Time to completion
- **Activities**: Timed challenges
- **Accuracy**: Must meet minimum accuracy threshold
- **Example**: Complete 10 lessons fastest with 80%+ score

### Hybrid Scoring
Combines multiple metrics:
- XP + Accuracy
- Speed + Quality
- Count + Performance

Formula varies by competition.

## Competition Lifecycle

### 1. Upcoming Phase
- **Status**: Not yet started
- **Actions**: View details, register, invite friends
- **Duration**: 1-7 days before start
- **Reminders**: 24h, 1h before start

### 2. Active Phase
- **Status**: In progress
- **Actions**: Compete, view leaderboard, track progress
- **Updates**: Real-time scoring
- **Notifications**: Rank changes, milestones

### 3. Completed Phase
- **Status**: Finished
- **Actions**: View results, claim prizes, review performance
- **Duration**: Results available indefinitely
- **Archive**: Historical data maintained

## Leaderboard Mechanics

### Update Frequency
- **Real-time**: Updated immediately for each activity
- **Batch**: Updated every 5-15 minutes (for large competitions)
- **Final**: Locked at competition end

### Rank Calculation
1. Primary metric (XP, count, accuracy, speed)
2. Tiebreakers (in order):
   - Total activities completed
   - Average score
   - Earlier completion time
   - Account creation date

### Display
- Top 100 shown in competition page
- User's personal rank always visible
- Rank change indicators (↑↓)
- Distance from next rank

### Privacy
- Users can opt-out of public display
- Opted-out users still compete for prizes
- Rankings adjusted for opted-out users

## Rules and Regulations

### Fair Play
1. **No Cheating**: Automated scripts, bots, or exploits prohibited
2. **No Account Sharing**: One person per account during competition
3. **No Manipulation**: Deliberately losing to benefit others banned
4. **Reporting**: Report suspicious activity via competition page

### Penalties
- **Warning**: First offense, minor violations
- **Disqualification**: Removed from competition, forfeit prizes
- **Suspension**: Temporary ban from competitions (7-30 days)
- **Permanent Ban**: Severe or repeated violations

### Dispute Resolution
1. Submit dispute within 24h of competition end
2. Provide evidence and explanation
3. Review by moderation team (3-5 business days)
4. Final decision communicated via email
5. Decision is final and binding

## Team Competitions

### Team Formation
- **Size**: 3-10 members
- **Creation**: Team leader creates and invites
- **Joining**: Accept invitation before competition start
- **Leaving**: Can leave until 24h before start

### Team Scoring
- **Aggregate**: Sum of all member scores
- **Average**: Average score across members
- **Best Performers**: Top N performers' scores (e.g., top 5)

Formula specified per competition.

### Team Prizes
- **Distribution**: Equal split among team members
- **MVP**: Top performer gets bonus
- **Leader**: Team leader gets small bonus

### Team Requirements
- All members meet minimum level
- Team registered before deadline
- Minimum team size maintained throughout

## Head-to-Head Competitions

### Challenge Creation
1. User A challenges User B
2. Set competition parameters:
   - Duration (1-7 days)
   - Activities included
   - Entry fee (optional)
   - Scoring method
3. User B accepts or declines
4. Competition begins on acceptance

### Prize Pool
- Both users contribute entry fee
- Winner takes 90% of pool
- 10% platform fee

### Rematch
- Loser can request rematch
- Winner can accept/decline
- New competition created if accepted

## Quiz Battles

### Real-Time Format
1. **Lobby**: Wait for opponents (30 seconds max)
2. **Match Start**: 5-10 questions
3. **Timed Answers**: 10-30 seconds per question
4. **Scoring**: Speed + accuracy
5. **Results**: Immediate, with ranking

### Scoring Formula
```
Score = (Correct Answers × 100) + (Time Bonus)
Time Bonus = (Time Remaining × 10)
```

### Matchmaking
- **Skill-Based**: Match similar level users
- **Quick Match**: First available opponents
- **Custom**: Invite specific users

## Best Practices

### For Participants

#### Before Competition
1. Review competition rules and scoring
2. Check entry requirements
3. Prepare study materials
4. Set personal goals
5. Invite friends (if team competition)

#### During Competition
1. Track your progress regularly
2. Maintain consistent daily effort (for multi-day)
3. Focus on quality over quantity
4. Take breaks to avoid burnout
5. Encourage teammates (if applicable)

#### After Competition
1. Review performance analytics
2. Identify areas for improvement
3. Celebrate achievements
4. Connect with top performers
5. Apply for rematch (if head-to-head)

### For Team Leaders
1. **Recruit Wisely**: Choose committed members
2. **Communicate**: Regular team updates
3. **Motivate**: Encourage and support
4. **Strategize**: Plan team approach
5. **Distribute Work**: Balance contributions

### For Platform Admins
1. **Balance Difficulty**: Appropriate challenge level
2. **Fair Prizes**: Reward matches effort
3. **Clear Rules**: Unambiguous guidelines
4. **Timely Updates**: Regular leaderboard refreshes
5. **Monitor**: Watch for cheating/exploits

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join multiple competitions simultaneously?**
A: Yes, participate in as many as you want.

**Q: What happens if I don't meet minimum activity requirements?**
A: You won't receive participation prizes but won't lose entry fee.

**Q: Can I leave a competition after joining?**
A: Yes, but entry fee is non-refundable (if applicable).

**Q: How are ties resolved?**
A: Via tiebreaker criteria listed in scoring section.

**Q: Can I use XP boosters during competitions?**
A: Yes, all boosters and multipliers apply.

**Q: Are competitions available on mobile?**
A: Yes, full competition functionality on all platforms.

**Q: How often are new competitions created?**
A: Weekly challenges every week, monthly tournaments every month, plus special events.

**Q: Can I view past competition results?**
A: Yes, complete history available in your profile.

**Q: What happens if there's a technical issue?**
A: Competition may be paused/extended. Affected users compensated.
